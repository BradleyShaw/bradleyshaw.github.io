About me.

Hi, I'm Bradley Shaw, I live in Manchester, UK and I'm a software developer proficient in Python and Node.js. I'm passionate about open source and IRC, I like to create and contribute to open source projects and chat on IRC as well as use it to communicate with other developers. Check out my projects and contributions on GitHub or see my contact page to find out how to get in touch with me.

When I'm not on my computer, you can usually find me riding or tinkering with motorcycles. I'm currently working on fixing a Kawasaki ER-5 which I bought as a project and intend to use once I pass my test. My favourite places to ride to/around are the Peak District, Cat & Fiddle and Forest of Bowland.
